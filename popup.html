How-to

Save
  • Click on 'New' tab. You will be able to see all open links that are opened in the browser.
  • Select the links that you want to save.
  • Once you have selected all the links, click on 'New session name' to create a new session OR click on 'Add to existing sessions' and select the session in which you want to add this links. Click 'Save'.
  • Ta daa..!
  • Click on 'Saved' tab to see all your saved sessions.
  • Click on Open to open all links in browser from that session.
